ARTIST STATEMENT

I am Chicago based Nepali artist. My work is about Nepali heritage and how we can bring our past into our future. My art is about revealing the simple things that normal vision doesn’t catch. My work highlights simple things with broad meanings; things we can see everywhere, but that we don’t usually think about. I always try to reveal the importance and beauty of common things.

I use traditional mediums like oil and acrylic color on canvas to express my feelings. On necessary, I also do installation works and take help of mix media to state social issues.

"Bhaikaji" Bishal Manandhar
